ğŸ® How to Play
Open the game in your browser.
Player X starts. Click on an empty cell to place your symbol.
Take turns with Player O, trying to align five symbols in any direction.
The game detects the winner and updates the score.
Click "Restart Game" to play again.
Player scores persist between sessions, so you can track your progress.
